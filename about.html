Our Story

A Legacy Built on Precision, Not Promises

Linkcore was founded on a straightforward conviction: every property deserves professional-grade surveillance. From our base in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, we have grown into a full-spectrum CCTV and cabling deployment company trusted by corporates, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and logistics operators alike.

Our certified engineers bring hands-on expertise across IP camera systems, structured cabling, NVR configurations, and video analytics. We do not cut corners. We deliver clean, documented, and reliable systems that keep watching long after our team leaves the site.

In 2026, Linkcore continues to lead with a proactive approach — embracing AI-assisted CCTV analytics, 4K ultra-HD surveillance, and cloud-based monitoring to future-proof every deployment.
