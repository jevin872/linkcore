Our Story

A Legacy Built on Precision, Not Promises

Linkcore was founded on a straightforward conviction: every business — regardless of size — deserves enterprise-grade IT infrastructure. From our base in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, we have grown into a full-spectrum technology deployment company trusted by corporates, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and logistics operators alike.

Our certified engineers bring hands-on expertise across structured cabling, IP surveillance, cybersecurity hardening, and server virtualisation. We do not cut corners. We do not overpromise. We deliver clean, documented, and scalable systems that keep running long after our team leaves the site.

In 2026, Linkcore continues to lead with a proactive approach — embracing Zero-Trust security frameworks, AI-assisted CCTV analytics, and cloud-hybrid server architectures to future-proof every deployment we touch.
