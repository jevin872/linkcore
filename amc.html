What Our CCTV AMC Guarantees

Our maintenance contracts are fully customizable based on your camera count and cabling infrastructure requirements.

Camera Lens Cleaning

Regular lens cleaning to ensure crystal-clear footage. Dust and debris removal from all camera housings.

Camera Angle Adjustment

Fine-tuning camera angles and focus for optimal coverage. Post-installation adjustments as your layout changes.

Emergency SLA Response

Onsite emergency response times guaranteed under 2 to 4 hours for camera failures and recording issues.

NVR/DVR Maintenance

Regular health checks of recording systems, firmware updates, and storage capacity monitoring.

Cable Inspection

Regular inspection of camera cables and connections. Identification and repair of damaged or degraded cabling.

Quarterly Reports

Complete transparency. We deliver quarterly summaries of camera health, storage status, and maintenance activities.
