What Our IT AMC Guarantees

Our contracts are fully customizable to align with your office node volumes and critical infrastructure requirements.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled onsite engineering visits to clean dust from servers, test battery backups, check switch ports, and inspect rack cabling connections.

Emergency SLA Action

Onsite emergency response times guaranteed under 2 to 4 hours. Dedicated support crew dispatch to remediate core connection drops or server fails.

Remote Helpline Support

Unlimited remote support sessions via TeamViewer or AnyDesk to resolve user software, printer connection errors, router bugs, or email setup problems.

CCTV Servicing

Routine lens cleaning, adjustment of camera angles, NVR firmware updates, and regular verification of storage disk recordings integrity.

Firewall Auditing

Installing security patches, monitoring intrusion threat logs, managing blacklists, and sorting user access groups on Sophos/Fortinet units.

Quarterly Auditing Reports

Complete transparency. We deliver quarterly summaries of internet speeds, firewall blocks, backup successes, and hardware degradation points.
